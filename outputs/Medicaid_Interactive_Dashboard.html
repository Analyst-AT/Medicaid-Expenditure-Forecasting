Methodology Notes

Train/Test Split: Models trained on FY 2013-2021, evaluated on FY 2022-2025.
Linear Regression: OLS on Year. Prediction intervals widen using t-distribution leverage formula.
Polynomial Regression (d=2): Quadratic OLS.
Holt's Exponential Smoothing: Level and trend parameters optimised by minimising SSE. CI widens as sqrt(h).
ARIMA(1,1,1): AR and MA parameters estimated by conditional least squares on first differences.
Best model selected by lowest MAPE on hold-out test set.
